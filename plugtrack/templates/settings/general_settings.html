Tips

Currency: This setting affects how all costs are displayed throughout the application, including session costs, charging rates, and savings calculations.

Savings Cards: These cards help you understand the practical impact of your EV charging sessions by comparing them to petrol costs.

Data Requirements: The savings cards will only appear when you have:

  • Configured petrol price and MPG in Cost & Efficiency settings
  • Session efficiency data available
  • Miles since previous session for comparison
